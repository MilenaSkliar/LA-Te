LA-Te uses a simple cookie preference banner to help visitors control whether they accept or decline non-essential cookies. This policy explains what cookies are, why they may be used, and how you can manage your choices.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help websites remember preferences, support basic functionality, and understand general website usage.

Similar technologies may also be used for performance, security, or preference-related purposes.

How We Use Cookies

LA-Te may use cookies to improve the website experience, remember cookie banner choices, and understand how visitors interact with the website in a general way.

Cookies may be used for purposes such as:

  • Remembering your cookie preference choice.
  • Supporting website functionality.
  • Helping maintain a smoother browsing experience.
  • Understanding general website performance and interaction patterns.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are used to support core website functionality. These may include cookies or local browser storage that remembers whether you accepted or declined the cookie banner.

These cookies are generally necessary for the website to work properly and to avoid showing the same preference message repeatedly after you have made a choice.

Analytics / Performance Cookies

Analytics or performance cookies may help understand how visitors use the website, which pages are visited, and how the website can be improved.

If such cookies are used, they are intended to support website improvement and user experience rather than identify individual visitors directly.

Managing Cookies

You can accept or decline cookies through the cookie banner displayed on the website. Once you choose an option, your preference may be stored in your browser through localStorage so the banner does not appear every time you visit.

You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect website functionality or preference storage.
